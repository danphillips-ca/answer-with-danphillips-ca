This was originally created for Hristo's Loyal Bison Slugs (est. 2022) - my trivia team in Halifax. Files that you load into this game are rendered completely by your browser; nothing is transferred from your computer.

Instructions

Use this template to populate trivia questions.

Download Template

Setup:

  • You can have up to four unique categories.
  • Questions (Q), Answers (A), Question Media (QM), and Answer Media (AM) must correspond to the same unique point value within their category. (See template.)
  • To include media, provide a complete online url. YouTube, images, audio, and video are generally supported. Ensure the websites hosting your trivia images allow hotlinking - for example, a public album on Google Photos. If you don't want media, leave those cells blank.
